The Best Movies According to Kyle

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Pulp Fiction

This is my Tarantino Film. Everytime I watch it I notice something different I felt as though I have never seen before.

Moonrise Kingdom

A feel good quirky film by Wes Anderson. It reminds me of how simple life can be.

Howl's Moving Castle

One of top animated films. Everything by Hayao Miyazaki is superb and this was my first introduction to him.
